description: I was the youngest grantee from India appointed to to carry out a campaign for road safety in my city. I charted out a plan deciding what activities my team could carry out and when. Eventually, a myriad of activities was organized from health checkups of over 70 school bus drivers and conductors to auditing the busiest roads (over 20) of my city. The audited data was uploaded to Google Maps. To create awareness among school students, we set up stalls in carnivals at different schools, and organized poster making and essay writing competitions (footfall of over 600 students), We also staged road plays during evenings to catch the general public's eye.
